About this website

Enter an artist name, and the website will generate a playlist of top songs of recommended artists based on your input. Fetches recommended artists from Spotify or Music-map depending on your choice. Also supports Spotify URIs (e.g. 'spotify:artist:xxxxxxx')

Using Music-map recommendations

When using Music-map for recommendations, playlist generation will take a slightly longer time (but you will usually get different recommendations).
